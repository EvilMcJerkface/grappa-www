Note: max is enforced only for blockable callers, and is enforced early to avoid callers that cannot block (callers from message handlers) to have a greater chance of proceeding.

In 'init_shared_pool()', 'emergency_alloc' margin is set, currently, to 1/4 of the max.

Currently setting this max to cap shared_pool usage at ~1GB, though this may be too much for some apps & machines, as it is allocated out of the locale-shared heap. It's also worth noting that under current settings, most apps don't reach this point at steady state.
